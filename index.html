About Me

Hi! I’m Nayara Pereira, PhD

Brazilian biologist, holder of a master’s and a PhD in immunology, and a programming enthusiast!

I personally believe that combining science and data in health research can take us far in understanding diseases. Working with clinical and biological data is something that truly motivates me, which is why I’ve been focusing on projects mainly in the health field.

When I’m not immersed in numbers and charts, you’ll probably find me surrounded by books, exploring art, piecing together puzzles, listening to podcasts, Rock music, or spending time with friends and my cat.
